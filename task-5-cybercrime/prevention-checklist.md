# Stay Safe Online Checklist for College Students in India

1.Use strong, unique passwords for all your online accounts and change them regularly.

2.Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible, especially on email and financial apps.

3.Avoid clicking on suspicious links or attachments in emails, messages, or social media.

4.Verify URLs carefully before entering personal or financial information—look for “https” and the padlock symbol.

5.Keep your devices and apps updated to protect against security vulnerabilities.

6.Be cautious while using public Wi-Fi—avoid accessing banking or sensitive sites on unsecured networks.

7.For UPI and online payments:
    *Always verify the payee’s details before confirming any transaction.
    *Use official banking or UPI apps downloaded from trusted sources like Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
    
8.Backup important data regularly and avoid sharing sensitive information on social media.

9.Report cybercrime immediately to the National Cyber Crime Portal at cybercrime.gov.in or call the helpline number 1930.
